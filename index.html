Utah, United States

The state of Utah was populated by various indigenous groups before Europeans began arriving in the mid 16th century. Later, near the mid 1800's, the Latter-Day Saints fled to Utah in large numbers and the area was annexed by the U.S. becoming the Utah Territory (which included colorado and Nevada). Utah was admitted as a state in 1896.
